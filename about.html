Sunvy

Hi my name is Sunvy and I enjoy playing and organizing tournaments for badminton, hiking, and video games.

My Favourite Interest: Badminton

Since I was little, my mother has always encouraged me to play badminton, which was her favorite sport. I participated in competitive badminton throughout middle school and high school. When I entered university, I joined the university badminton club as the VP of events. The club was partnered with the provincial badminton association, and thus I was asked to work with the organization to host a prestigious international competition. Ever since 2019, I have been working annually with the organization to host badminton open competitions. Last year, we had the five legends of badminton: Lee Chong Wei, Lin Dan, Peter Gade, Taufik Hidayat, and Lee Yong Dae attend an event during the competition.
